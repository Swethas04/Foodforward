About Us

At FoodForward, we are dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting hunger. Our platform connects food donors with organizations that distribute meals to those in need.

We believe that no one should go to bed hungry while perfectly good food goes to waste. By donating surplus food, you help create a sustainable future and make a real impact in your community.

Join us in this mission to feed the hungry and minimize food waste.
